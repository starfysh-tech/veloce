Company Formation

Where we are

Veloce is at the pre-formation stage — the MVP exists, the founding team is in place, and entity formation is the immediate next step.

Formation is planned as a Delaware C-corp, the standard structure for US VC-track startups. The founding team is a US/Canada cross-border pair, which adds complexity around tax structure, withholding, and reporting obligations — so professional advisors (cross-border tax counsel and a US startup lawyer) are engaged before any documents are filed.

Formation proceeds in parallel with the product roadmap. It runs through four broad phases: founder decisions on equity, IP, and structure; structural decisions with professionals; the mechanical incorporation steps; and ongoing compliance setup. The Starfysh-to-Veloce IP transfer and the Canadian co-founder's holding structure are the two items with the most formation-time consequence and are treated as professional decisions, not checklist items.

Formation and product work are independent tracks — neither blocks the other. The one point of coordination is the potential regulatory classification (Roadmap Track 1), which can influence corporate structure and is flagged to counsel in Phase 1.

Full detail — phases, sequencing, and the cross-border compliance traps — in docs/COMPANY-FORMATION.md.
