Guided tour

Follow this sequence to walk through the full workflow end-to-end.

  1. Dana (Trader) — log in and launch a new RFQ. Set terms, select dealers, and submit for approval.
  2. Marcus (Approver) — review the pending RFQ and approve it to release to dealers.
  3. Tomás (Ops) — once the auction closes and a dealer is awarded, capture the trade and affirm it.
  4. Ingrid (Compliance) — review the best-execution record and concentration dashboard.
  5. Alex (Admin) — explore firm administration: bank panels, user management, audit views.
  6. Priya (Halcyon) — log in as a user of the second firm. Verify that Meridian's RFQs and data are not visible — this confirms tenant isolation.

How dealers participate

Dealers do not hold platform accounts. When an RFQ is released, each invited dealer receives a one-time magic link scoped to that specific auction. Following the link opens a blinded quoting interface — no login required, no cross-RFQ visibility. The buy-side desk shares the link directly with the dealer contact.
