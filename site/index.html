The control problem

A treasury desk hedging a large equity book can collect dealer evidence across calls, chat, email, and spreadsheets. Liquidity, award rationale, policy exceptions, approvals, and best-execution evidence can be hard to compare, govern, and reconstruct under review.

What Veloce does

Veloce provides a parallel-run control and evidence layer: invited dealers quote the same mock pilot request at the same time; the desk sees a ranked board, an illustrative single-bank vs. blended-allocation comparison, concentration and threshold checks, approval governance, a prepared payload for operations review, and compliance evidence — each step written to an append-only audit log beside execution.
