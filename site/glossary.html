Glossary

Key terms as used in the Veloce platform, written for a reader without a derivatives background.

Term Definition
RFQ Request for Quote — the buy-side firm's formal solicitation sent to one or more dealers asking them to price a specific trade.
OTC equity derivative A privately negotiated contract whose value is derived from an equity index or basket, traded directly between two parties rather than on a public exchange.
Auction In Veloce, a time-boxed competitive process in which multiple dealers submit quotes for the same RFQ; the platform surfaces the best price to the buy-side desk for award.
Dealer / Bank A financial institution (typically an investment bank) that prices and takes the other side of the trade; in Veloce, dealers respond to auctions via a one-time magic link rather than a standing account.
Best execution The obligation to obtain the most favorable terms reasonably available when executing a trade on a client's behalf; Veloce records the full quote board and award rationale to support this audit trail.
STP Straight-Through Processing — automated transmission of trade details from execution directly into downstream systems (OMS, custodian, clearinghouse) without manual re-keying.
Affirmation The post-execution step in which both the buy-side and the dealer formally confirm that the trade terms match; in Veloce this is the Ops role's task after award.
Concentration The degree to which a portfolio's notional exposure is clustered in a small number of dealers or instruments; Veloce surfaces concentration metrics to the Compliance role as a risk governance tool.
Notional The face-value size of a derivative trade — the reference amount on which payoffs are calculated, not the premium paid upfront.
Blind auction An auction format in which competing dealers cannot see each other's quotes during the live phase; Veloce enforces this by serving each dealer a masked view of the quote board.
Capability token A short-lived, opaque credential issued to a dealer for a single RFQ; it grants access to that auction's quoting interface without requiring a platform account or password.
